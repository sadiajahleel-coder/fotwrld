## Background

Factories of The World (FOTWRLD) was conceived at the intersection of artisanal craft and industrial ambition.

The platform combines:
- field research
- interviews
- workflow mapping
- exhibitions
- publications

to understand how decentralised production systems function.

## Context

Nigeria’s artisanal economy holds significant industrial potential but remains structurally invisible.

Jewelry was chosen as the starting point due to its position between:
- raw material extraction
- global luxury markets

## Institutional Positioning

FOTWRLD operates in partnership with:
- National Commission for Museums and Monuments (NCMM)
- Ahmadu Bello University (ABU)
- Government agencies

This positions the platform at the intersection of:
**culture + enterprise**
