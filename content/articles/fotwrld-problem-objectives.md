## Problem Statement

Nigeria’s artisanal sector faces a structural gap between:

- creative capacity  
- economic visibility  

Key issues include:

- Invisibility to investors  
- Limited access to tools  
- Weak institutional integration  
- Decline in heritage transmission  
- Underutilised cultural institutions  

## Objectives

FOTWRLD’s work is structured around:

1. Document artisan businesses  
2. Build incubation infrastructure  
3. Create investment-facing publications  
4. Translate research into exhibitions  
5. Develop operational systems  
6. Position FOTWRLD as a cultural economy partner
