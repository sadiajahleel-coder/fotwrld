## Research Overview

**Organisation:** Factories of The World (FOTWRLD)  
**Lead Researcher:** Halima Abdul (Sadia)  
**Location:** Kaduna, Nigeria  
**Period:** 2025–2026  

Factories of The World (FOTWRLD) is an independent research and production platform operating at the intersection of cultural heritage and industrial economy.

This report documents one year of engagement across research, exhibitions, publications, and institutional partnerships.

## What This Report Covers

This series explores:

- Artisan economies in Nigeria  
- Micro-factory production systems  
- Institutional partnerships  
- Investment readiness  
- Cultural economy infrastructure  

## Core Question

> How do we make Nigeria’s artisans visible, investable, and sustainable?
