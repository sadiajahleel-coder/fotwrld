## Methodology

FOTWRLD employs a field-first, practitioner-led research methodology.

Research is conducted with artisans, not on them.

## Core Principles

- Practitioner-led inquiry  
- Case-study format  
- Multi-format outputs  
- Institutional anchoring  
- Action-oriented research  

## Methods Used

- Long-form interviews  
- Studio observation  
- Exhibition documentation  
- Institutional collaboration  

## Frameworks Applied

- Circular Economy Analysis  
- Investment Readiness Assessment  
- Cultural Economy Theory  
- Case Study Methodology
